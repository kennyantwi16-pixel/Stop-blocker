Privacy Policy — stop blocker

Effective Date: [12/11/2025]

stop blocker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal user data. All functionality runs locally on your device.

Local Storage

The extension may store user settings locally (for example, the blocked-site list and premium flag). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Payments

Payment processing for premium subscriptions is handled entirely by Paystack; payment details are handled by Paystack and not stored by this extension.

Data Deletion

Remove settings by uninstalling the extension or clearing local extension data in Chrome.
