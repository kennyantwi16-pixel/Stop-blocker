Privacy Policy — Stop Blocker

Effective Date: 12/11/2025

Stop Blocker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal user data. All functionality runs locally on your device.

Local Storage

The extension may store user settings locally (for example, the blocked-site list and premium flag). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Payments

Payment processing for premium subscriptions is handled entirely by Paystack. Payment details are handled securely by Paystack and are not stored by this extension.

Data Deletion

You can remove all extension data by uninstalling the extension or clearing local extension storage in Chrome.
